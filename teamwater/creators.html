Overview

Who We Are

Staple Games is a 4-person Austin mobile game studio that strives to #PlayItForward by using our 20+ mobile games to raise money for great causes. Last month Ryan Trahan's fans donated $815,000 to St. Jude without opening their wallets by playing our games during his 50 States in 50 Days YouTube series.

What We Are Doing

Like you, we've joined #TeamWater to help raise $40M to provide clean water for 2 million people for decades. We are releasing Match Collector "Water World" where every Water Tile collected = 1 week of clean water donated. We're partnering with creators to amplify this play-to-give campaign and incorporating custom Creator Trophies to attribute your audience's gameplay donations to your #TeamWater community.

How You Can Help

Amplify the game launch starting August 13 so your audience can give without opening their wallets. If you'd like your collectible tile in the game, send us some inspiration. We'll do the heavy lifting and design it for you! We're committing at least $150,000 to #TeamWater, with daily donations tallied and attributed to your Creator Communities on Tiltify so your fans can track their impact!

The Mission

To give your audience a free way to contribute to #TeamWater we are releasing a special edition of our popular Match Collector Game. In addition to donating a month of water per download, Water World includes custom tiles and Creator Trophies that unlock an extra week of water every time they are collected!

Visit staplegames.com

Give Your Fans More Ways to Donate

Many viewers want to help #TeamWater but can't donate cash. Our games give them a zero-wallet path to contribute in a meaningful way. Every play adds up - our last campaign generated over $815,000 for St. Jude!
