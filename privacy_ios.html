Overview

This app is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data. All app functionality runs entirely on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information or usage data.

The only information we may receive is aggregated, optional analytics provided by Apple, but only if you have chosen to share this information with app developers in your Apple device settings.

  • This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you.
  • We do not have access to any raw personal data from your device.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no information for us to use, share, or sell.

Aggregated Apple-provided analytics (if you opt in) may be used only to understand app performance and improve stability.

Data Sharing

We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect any personal data.

Your Choices

You can choose whether or not to share anonymous analytics with app developers using your Apple device settings:

Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements → Share with App Developers.
