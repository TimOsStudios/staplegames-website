Summary. This policy covers two games — Block Puzzle and Calculator — that Staple Games distributes through lock screen experiences. The games measure how they're being used so we can improve them. They do not collect your name, phone number, location, contacts, or carrier account information, and they do not show ads.

Who we are

Staple Games, LLC
13750 San Pedro Ave, Suite 810
San Antonio, TX 78232
Contact: support@staplegames.com

What we collect

  • Session events — when the game opens, closes, and re-opens, with timestamps.
  • A pseudonymous installation ID — a random number stored on your device, used only to recognize the same device returning. Not linked to any personal identifier.
  • Engagement events — screen views, game starts and ends, and feature interactions, used to evaluate game design.
  • Diagnostic events — errors and load failures, used to fix bugs.
  • Truncated IP address — incidental to standard network requests, used to estimate region only. The last segment is dropped before storage.

What we don't collect

  • Your name, phone number, email, or mailing address.
  • Your carrier account information, subscriber ID, MSISDN, or precise location.
  • Your contacts, calendar, photos, or files.
  • The numbers you type into the calculator, the moves you make in the puzzle, or any other input or result.
  • Advertising IDs, fingerprints, or cookies.

Why we collect it

  • To measure usage and whether players come back.
  • To find and fix bugs.
  • To evaluate and improve game design.

Not used for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

Who receives it

  • Google LLC processes the events listed above on our behalf through Google Analytics for Firebase. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out of Google Analytics via Google's opt-out browser add-on.
  • AppsFlyer Inc. receives a click record (timestamp, IP, OS) if you tap a "Play Free Now" link, to measure how many lock-screen players install the full app. AppsFlyer's privacy policy: appsflyer.com/legal/services-privacy-policy.

We do not share data with anyone else.

Do we sell or share?

No. We do not sell or share your personal information with anyone for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose, as those terms are defined under California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, and similar US state privacy laws.

How long we keep it

  • Analytics events: up to 14 months.
  • Diagnostic events: up to 30 days.
  • Aggregated anonymous statistics: indefinitely (no individual data).

Your rights

If you are a US resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what we have about you.
  • Delete what we have about you.
  • Correct what we have.
  • Opt out of any sale or sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising (we don't do this).
  • Limit use of sensitive personal information (we don't collect any).
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email support@staplegames.com. Because we collect only pseudonymous data, we may need additional information to locate your records.

We honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) browser signal as an opt-out request.

Children

The games are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the games and we have collected data about them, email support@staplegames.com and we will delete it.

Changes

We will post any changes to this policy here. The effective date at the top reflects the current version. Material changes will be summarized at the top of the page for at least 30 days after publication.
