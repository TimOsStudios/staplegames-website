50 Stays for St. Jude Campaign Terms

Official Terms and Conditions

Last updated: September 19, 2025

Our promise in plain English: From September 20 through December 31, 2025 we'll donate $0.02 for every Event Tile (a "State," "Stay," or "Roadtrip" tile) collected in Match Collector, and $0.10 for every St. Jude Tile collected. Donations will continue until we hit our $1,000,000 cap. Thank you for helping the kids of St. Jude!

1. Who we are

Staple Games, LLC. (called "Staple Games," "we," or "us").

2. Campaign Period

Starts: 10:00 a.m. Central Time (CT) September 20, 2025
Ends: 11:59 p.m. CT December 31, 2025

We may extend the end date or restart the campaign if community response is amazing (§8).

3. How the donation works

  • Event Tile donation amount: $0.02 per Event Tile (State, Stay, or Roadtrip) collected.
  • St. Jude Tile donation amount: $0.10 per St. Jude Tile collected.
  • Overall cap: $1,000,000 total, inclusive of all donations to St. Jude from the 50 States campaign.
  • Optional Boosts: We may announce short-term "Boosts" to increase the per-tile donation rate or add special bonuses. Boost details will be posted on our website and social channels.

If we extend the campaign or raise the cap, we'll announce it publicly.

4. What counts as a "Tile"?

A tile counts when all of these are true:

  1. It is a special Event Tile collected inside Match Collector during the Campaign Period; and
  2. Our analytics platform records the tile collection as genuine (not a bot, duplicate, emulator, VPN farm, or other fraudulent traffic).

5. Eligibility & "No purchase necessary"

Anyone, anywhere can generate donations—no payment, purchase, or in-app spend is required. Void where prohibited by law.

6. How we count and remit donations

  • Weekly estimate: At least each week we'll pull the previous day's Event and St. Jude Tile counts and immediately donate that estimated amount to St. Jude.
  • Reconciliation: Within 30 days after the campaign ends we'll reconcile against final analytics data and donate any additional amount owed.
  • Transparency: We'll update a public running total at staplegames.com/50-stays.

7. Privacy

We log anonymous device IDs, gameplay timestamps, and tile-collection data to verify activity and fight fraud. For details, read our Privacy Policy. This Campaign also incorporates and is subject to our Terms of Use except where those Terms conflict with these Campaign Terms. We do not sell your data.

8. Good-faith adjustments

If technical issues, fraud, or other factors threaten the integrity of the campaign, we may pause, adjust, or end it early. We'll act in good faith and post any changes at the link above.

9. Independent promotion

Apple Inc. and Google LLC are not sponsors or participants in this promotion.

10. Governing law

These Terms are governed by the laws of the State of Texas, USA.

11. Contact

Questions? Email support@staplegames.com.

Thank you for playing—and for helping St. Jude's mission of finding cures and saving children.
